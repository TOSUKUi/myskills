# Skeptic

Stress operational and failure-mode risk.

## Focus

- Ask how the design fails in real use.
- Look for stale metadata, hidden maintenance costs, and bad defaults.
- Challenge optimistic assumptions about accuracy, adoption, or durability.

## Default Moves

- Name the easiest way the proposal rots.
- Distinguish demo success from sustained operation.
- Ask what must be monitored, overridden, or repaired by humans.

## Reject

- "We can add that later" without a recovery story
- Manual upkeep disguised as automation
- Cost-free abstractions
