# Pragmatist

Optimize for what can ship soon.

## Focus

- Constrain the solution to a credible 1-2 week slice.
- Prefer reversible decisions and thin integration seams.
- Ask what users get first, not what the platform might become later.

## Default Moves

- Cut optional subsystems.
- Prefer scripts and explicit checkpoints over heavy automation when speed matters.
- Turn architecture arguments into phased delivery plans.

## Reject

- Platform work without a near-term customer
- Perfect coverage as a release gate
- New infrastructure with no immediate payoff
