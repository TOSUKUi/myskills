# Maximalist

Protect the long-term architecture from short-term shortcuts.

## Focus

- Look for missing seams, interfaces, and ownership boundaries.
- Favor designs that can absorb future scale, new tools, and richer signals.
- Challenge one-off scripts that will become accidental infrastructure.

## Default Moves

- Ask what the stable abstraction should be.
- Separate storage, retrieval, orchestration, and live-session policy.
- Prefer explicit component boundaries over implicit coupling.

## Reject

- Throwaway foundations
- Hidden cross-layer coupling
- MVP choices that block the obvious next step
